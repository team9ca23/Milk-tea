Our story

We have opened multiple stores in China and Finland, where we use fresh fruits daily to create delightful beverages.

In China, we collaborate with local farmers for the freshest seasonal produce, promoting a healthy lifestyle.

In Finland, we blend Asian beverage culture with local flavors, offering unique handcrafted drinks in relaxing spaces.

Committed to quality, we aim to provide customers with surprising and refreshing beverage experiences, celebrating the joy of fresh and healthy living in every sip.
